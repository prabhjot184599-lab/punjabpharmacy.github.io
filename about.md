Welcome to **Punjab Pharmacy**, the premier digital portal dedicated exclusively to aspiring pharmacy professionals in Punjab.

### Our Mission
Our core mission is simple: to provide high-yield, deeply researched, and strategically targeted study material for the upcoming **Punjab Drug Inspector (DI) 2026** exam and BFUHS Pharmacist recruitment boards. 

We know that clearing highly competitive state-level exams requires more than just reading textbooks—it requires studying the *right* concepts. That is why we focus our efforts on breaking down complex pharmaceutical data into digestible notes, clear classifications, and rigorous daily practice questions.

### What We Provide
* **High-Yield Notes:** Streamlined material focusing on heavily tested subjects like Pharmacology and Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence.
* **Daily MCQ Bank:** Previous Year Questions (PYQs) and strictly curated mock questions matching the exact difficulty level of state exams.
* **Exam Updates:** Timely alerts regarding syllabus changes, recruitment notifications, and study schedules.
